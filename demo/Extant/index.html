TwoFaas - Your Two-Factor Login System

TwoFaas is a secure extension tool to easily deploy and enable two factor authentication on your system

Two Factor Authentication Is Important

Two Factor Authentication helps secure your users by preventing destructive hacks and identity theft.

However, every startup and organization must create their own unique solution. Enter our API

Features Of TwoFaas

  • Easy to Implement

    With plugins for numerous frameworks, the TwoFaas SDK works easily with your existing login system and can be fully deployed in less than five minutes.

  • Secure

    We use advanced cryptography to handle all the complicated second factor authorization. All data and connections are encrypted. Your privacy and security is guaranteed.

  • Full Customization

    The design and user flow of TwoFaas is fully customizable. You know your users best, so we let you interact with them directly.
