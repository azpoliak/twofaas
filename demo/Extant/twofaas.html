Two Factor Authentication Is Important

Two Factor Authentication helps secure your users by preventing destructive hacks and identity theft

Reasons for Lack of Two Factor Authentication

  • Two Factor is Hard

    In theory, two factor authentication is a simple solution but in practice, two factor authentication is a technically hard problem to solve.

  • Limited Resources

    Start Ups are busy with building their product and features and do not have the time needed to implement, support, and maintain two factor authentication

  • Fully Customization

    The design and user flow of TwoFaas is fully customizalible. You know your users best so we let you interact with them directly.
